Design Principles

Core Web Design Principles

Typography

Good typography makes your content readable and pleasant to look at. Use no more than three font styles in your design, and ensure the text color contrasts well with its background. Clear, consistent font usage builds trust and professionalism across your website.

Usability Checklist

  • Use simple and consistent navigation.
  • Keep color contrast above 4.5:1 for readability.
  • Include alt text for all images.
  • Use whitespace to separate sections clearly.
  • Make sure buttons and links provide feedback when clicked.

Accessibility and Color

Accessible design ensures everyone can use your site comfortably, including people with visual impairments. High-contrast colors and keyboard navigation are essential. Always test your pages with accessibility tools like WAVE or Lighthouse to verify compliance with WCAG standards.
