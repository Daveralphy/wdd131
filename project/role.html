My Role at Swift Insight

I work as the Sales Development Representative focused on finding the right leads, starting conversations, and supporting the pipeline with qualified opportunities.

Work With Me Back Home

Daily Responsibilities

Prospect Research

Use LinkedIn Sales Navigator to search for companies and decision makers that match the ideal customer profile.

Outbound Outreach

Send personalized connection requests and follow up messages that feel relevant, not automated or spammy.

Lead Qualification

Ask simple questions to learn interest and fit before passing a lead forward.

Pipeline Support

Update lead records so the team can see who has been contacted, who replied, and who needs follow up.

Content Posting

Help prepare and publish short posts that share wins or useful information on the company page.

Comment Engagement

Respond to comments on leadership posts and keep conversations going.

Tools I Use

LinkedIn Sales Navigator

Search and filter for companies and contacts that match the ideal customer profile.

CRM

Keep track of conversations, qualification status, and follow up tasks so nothing is missed.

Leadership Profiles

Support visibility by posting content and engaging with reactions and comments on the CEO and company page.
