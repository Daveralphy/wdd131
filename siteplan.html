Swift Insight Portfolio Site Plan

Site Name

The name for this project is Swift Insight Portfolio. I wanted something that directly reflects the work I do at Swift Insight and presents it in a clear, professional way.

Site Purpose

This site explains Swift Insight’s services and showcases the responsibilities I manage in my role, including CRM setup, outreach workflows, reporting, and automation processes.

Scenarios

  • A business owner visits the site to learn how Swift Insight improves lead generation.
  • A visitor wants to understand my daily tasks and workflow at Swift Insight.
